Rules used

  1. In-center dialysis: ESRD entitlement begins the 1st day of the fourth month following in‑center dialysis treatment starting.
  2. Home dialysis: ESRD entitlement begins after home dialysis training begins.
  3. Transplant with no dialysis history: entitlement begins the 1st day of the month the patient is hospitalized for transplant surgery.
  4. ESRD Coordination of Benefits: the 30-month COB period ends on the last day of the 30th month following the initial ESRD entitlement date.
  5. Successful kidney transplant: ESRD-based entitlement ends on the last day of the 36th month after the month of transplant.
Educational calculator only. Verify unusual cases separately, especially graft loss, return to dialysis, retransplant, or separate Medicare entitlement due to age or disability.
